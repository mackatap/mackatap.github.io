The game

SUPASOK is a 3d platformer with an entirely new input method. Using just one finger you can run freely in 3d space, jump, flip, dive, crouch, and much more!

SUPASOK also has a level editor with over 25 different types of blocks to create huge variety of levels. Share your levels online and compete with other users for high scores.
