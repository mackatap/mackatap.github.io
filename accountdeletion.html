Steps to delete Supasok Maker 3d account.

Account Deletion can be completed through the app by logging into online, tapping the account tools button, and pressing delete.

Alternatively, email mackatap@gmail.com using the email used to sign up. Include "DELETE ACCOUNT" in the email subject.

All data associated with the account will be removed from the database. This includes uploaded levels, highscores, and any account details such as username, tickets, and purchased shop items.
